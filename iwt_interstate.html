About the Festival




The Discovery Experience

Come & experience Tasmania's wines and food at their best. With live music throughout the day, follow the trail through the scenic gardens to find Tasmania's best wine makers, waiting to give you rare insights into their wine & wine making.

A Full Palate Awaits

Featuring over 30 Tasmanian vineyards and wines from all around Tasmania, Some of the best Tassie foodies will compliment your drop of choice with delicious food. Perhaps an oyster to enjoy with your sparkling, a burger or pizza with your red.
