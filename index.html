IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT


Tas Wine Fest — Winter Edition has been cancelled.
Refunds will go out over Oztix immediately.

It has become apparent that we can’t make a winter Tas Wine Fest work this year, since tepid support from the state government has made the festival economically unviable. It’s not the outcome we wanted, and our thoughts go out to our industry partners affected by this unfortunate news.

Please contact info@taswinefest.com.au for questions, and Oztix for refund queries.

At this time, we’re hopeful that the government will get behind Tas Wine Fest in the summer, and will look to support a winter event in 2022.
