Masterclasses

Learn from the best

Add to your Tasmanian Wine Festival session intimate wine focused classes bring together the best experts in Tasmania, including Joseph Burton (Tasmania’s first certified Sommelier), Carl Winsdor (Willing Bros.) and a yet to be announced expert from Frogmore Creek.



Savour your senses

Each Masterclass session will get tastings of a handpicked selection of Tasmania’s wines, paired with a gourmet selection of Bruny Island Oysters, cheeses and other delights.

Each Class has a maximum of 10 attendees for an intimate experience.
