ENTERTAINMENT

Dance the afternoon away, or make yourself comfortable on the grass with our great line up of local entertainment, taking to the stage and roaming throughout the festival.




The Whispering Jack Show

Appearing at all sessions

Australia's premier tribute to John Farnham, this powerhouse production from Mike Vee celebrates the songs that defined a generation. Featuring a full band and soaring vocals, it brings Farnham's iconic catalogue to life.

Hobart Funk Collective

Appearing at all sessions

Hobart Funk Collective brings together some of Tasmania's finest musicians in an eleven piece band dedicated to all things funk, soul and groove.

Powered by four horns, two guitars, bass, drums and percussion, their feel good sound is rooted in classic funk and refined with a contemporary edge, as the group reunites at the Tasmanian Wine Festival.
