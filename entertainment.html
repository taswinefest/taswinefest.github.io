Dance the afternoon away, or make yourself comfortable on the grass with our great line up of local entertainment, taking to the stage and roaming throughout the festival.




Melbourne Ska Orchestra

Appearing at all sessions

The 22 peice juggernaut that is Melbourne Ska Orchestra contains some of Australia’s finest musicians and performers.

Helmed by the energetic and charismatic Nicky Bomba (John Butler Trio, Bustamento), they're more than just a band, they are an fun-loving experience to behold.

Boil Up

Appearing at all sessions

A melting pot of contemporary Australia, Boil Up's roots stretch from the traditional owners of Tasmania, New Zealand and Fiji, to as far away as Ireland, England and Madagascar.

Featuring rich harmonies blended together with traditional reggae, funk and R&B, the ten peice have created their own unique sound of reggae fusion.
