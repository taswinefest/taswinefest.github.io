Frequently Asked Questions

    When & Where

  • Where's the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held at the Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens in Hobart, Tasmania.

    Access to the event is via the main gates on Lower Domain Road. There is no entry or parking at the bottom carpark on the Domain Highway.

  • When is the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held annually in summer. The 2025 edition will come to the Botanical Gardens, Hobart on Feb 8 & 9.

    • Tickets

  • How much does it cost?

    Early Bird tickets start at $23. More pricing information is available here

  • How do I purchase tickets?

    Tickets can be purchased through Oztix our authorised ticket agency.

    Tickets should only purchased through the authorised ticket provider. Tickets brought from any other platform cannot be guaranteed as legitimate.

    Patrons with fraudulent tickets will not be admitted.

  • Can I purchase tickets on the door?

    If there are still tickets available, these will be available for purchase.

    At previous editions, multiple sessions sold out prior to the event, so pre-purchasing tickets is highly recommended.

    Tickets on the door are sold at a premium.

  • Do I need to print my tickets?

    We encorage patrons to keep their tickets on mobile devices.

  • Is this an accessible event?

    We encourage all patrons with accessibility requirements to contact the festival team prior to the event: info@taswinefest.com.au

  • Do you accept companion cards?

    Yes! The Tasmanian Wine Festival accepts valid Companion Cards. Companion card holders are required to contact the festival prior to the event: info@taswinefest.com.au

    Companions must arrive with the ticket holder they are with.

  • What does a Tasting ticket include

    Tasting Tickets to the Tasmanian Wine Festival include general admission to the festival and tasting tokens upon arrival.

    Tasting Tickets are 18+

  • What wines will be available?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival has a wide range reds, whites and sparklings from all around the state at the event, including both Tasmanian staples and varietals you may not know of. Check out all of our vendors here

    In addition to some great Tassie wines, a range of summer cocktails, craft beers, cider, spirits and non-alcoholic beverages will be available.

    Check out the ticketing page for VIP ticket inclusions

    VIP Tickets are 18+

  • Is this an all ages event?

    The Saturday Afternoon and Sunday sessions are Licensed All Ages, all other sessions are 18+.

    Patrons under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian above the age of 25.

    Children aged between 5-17 years must hold a ticket to gain entry. Under 5s are free and do not require a ticket.

    Ticket holders under the age of 18 cannot purchase a Tasting or VIP ticket.

  • Is there food available?

    Yes. We have a beautiful range of food available, also including vegan, vegetarian, gluten free and dairy free options. See the feasting line-up here

  • Are there beverages available other than wine?

    We have a wide variety of beverages available from craft beers,ciders, spirits and summer cocktails. This is in addition to a wide range of non alcoholic drinks.
