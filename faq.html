Frequently Asked Questions

    When & Where

  • Where is the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held at the Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens in Hobart, Tasmania.

    Access to the event is via the main gates on Lower Domain Road. There is no entry or parking at the bottom carpark on the Domain Highway.

  • When is the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held annually in summer. The 2025 edition will come to the Botanical Gardens, Hobart on Feb 8 & 9.

    Sign up for the latest news before anyone else here

  • How do I get there?

    Entry to the event is through the Main Gates of the Botanical Gardens only, which is located on Lower Doamin Road.

    There is limited parking available on Lower Domain Road (near the Main Entrance of the Botanical Gardens), as well as a few car parks surrounding the Gardens. We encourage patrons to get dropped off or use public transportation including Taxis and rideshare vehicles.

    The bottom carpark on the Domain Highway is closed off for the public. There is no parking or entry via this gate.

  • Where should I stay?

    If you are travelling from out of town, Hobart has some great accommodation options available. Check out our recommendations below:

    RACV Hotel Hobart
    The Tasman, a Luxury Collection Hotel
    IBIS Styles Hobart
    Mövenpick Hotel Hobart

    Make sure to get in quick, Hobart is very busy at this time of year.

    • Tickets

  • How much does it cost?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is a ticketed event. Early Bird tickets start at $23. More pricing information is available here

  • How do I purchase tickets?

    Tickets can be purchased through Oztix, our authorised ticket agency.

    Tickets should only purchased through the authorised ticket provider. Tickets brought from any other platform cannot be guaranteed as legitimate.

    Patrons with fraudulent tickets will not be admitted.

  • Can I purchase tickets on the door?

    If there are still tickets available, these will be available for purchase.

    At previous editions, multiple sessions sold out prior to the event, so pre-purchasing tickets is highly recommended.

    Tickets on the door are sold at a premium.

  • Is this an accessible event?

    We encourage all patrons with accessibility requirements to contact the festival team prior to the event: info@taswinefest.com.au

  • Do you accept companion cards?

    Yes! The Tasmanian Wine Festival accepts valid Companion Cards. Companion card holders are required to contact the festival prior to the event: info@taswinefest.com.au

    Companions must arrive with the ticket holder they are with.

    • The Event

  • What do I get?

    • Access to Tasmania's best vineyards in one location
    • Heaps of local food options
    • Live music from great bands & artists
    • Premium reuseable wine glass

    Purchasing a Tasting Ticket brings the benefits of a General Admission ticket, along with Tasting Tokens.

  • What can/can't I bring?


    What can you bring:

    • Picnic Rugs
    • Low lying chairs - for use at the rear of the stage viewing area only. Festival staff may request chairs be moved if they impede the view of others
    • Empty reusable water bottles. There are free water refill stations available
    • Sun screen, a hat, and a warm sweater/jumper for the evenings

    What can't you bring:

    • Food & Alcohol. This is a strictly no BYO event.
    • Animals, with the exception of assistance animals
    • Glassware. All ticket holders receive a tumbler on entry
    • Weapons, fireworks, illicit substances, pets
    • Professional film equipment
    • Marketing materials of any kind

  • What wines will be available?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival has a wide range reds, whites and sparklings from all around the state at the event, including both Tasmanian staples and varietals you may not know of. Check out all of our vendors here

    In addition to some great Tassie wines, a range of summer cocktails, craft beers, cider, spirits and non-alcoholic beverages will be available.

  • Is this an all ages event?

    The Saturday Afternoon and Sunday sessions are Licensed All Ages, all other sessions are 18+.

    Patrons under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian above the age of 25.

    Children aged between 5-17 years must hold a ticket to gain entry. Under 5s are free and do not require a ticket.

    Ticket holders under the age of 18 cannot purchase a Tasting or VIP ticket.

  • Is there food available?

    Yes. We have a beautiful range of food available, also including vegan, vegetarian, gluten free and dairy free options. See the feasting line-up here

  • Are there beverages available other than wine?

    We have a wide variety of beverages available from craft beers,ciders, spirits and summer cocktails. This is in addition to a wide range of non alcoholic drinks.

    • Tastings

  • Are there tastings?

    Most vineyards at the Tasmanian Wine Festival offer tastings to patrons. Tastings can be redeemed by purchasing Tasting Tokens that are available on the day, or included with a Tasting Ticket or VIP Ticket.

    Like what you tried? Make sure to purchase a glass or bottle to support Tasmanian wine producers & vineyards!

    There are no free tastings at the Tasmanian Wine Festival.

  • Are there free tastings?

    No. Vendors at the Tasmanian Wine Festival do not offer free tastings.

    Tasting Tokens are available with either a Tasting or VIP Ticket, or can be purchased at the event.

    Like what you tried? Make sure to purchase a glass or bottle to support Tasmanian wine producers & vineyards!

    • Reserved Tables

  • What is a Reserved Table?

    Reserved Tables at the Tasmanian Wine Festival offer groups a guaranteed reserved table at their chosen event session.

    Reserved Tables are available for groups of up to six people.

  • Do I also need an event ticket if I have a Reserved Table?

    Yes. Reserved Tables do not provide access to the event.

    Each person sitting at a reserved table requires their own event ticket.

  • Where will my table be?

    Your table location will be provided to you closer to the event date.
