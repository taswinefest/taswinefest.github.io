Frequently Asked Questions

    When & Where

  • Where's the event?

    The Winter Edition of the Tasmanian Wine Festival is held at The Goods Shed in Hobart, Tasmania.

    Our Summer Edition is held annually at the Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens.

  • What are the session times?

    Saturday Afternoon: 12pm - 4.30pm
    Saturday Evening: 5.30pm - 10pm
    Sunday Afternoon 12 - 5.30pm

    Tickets are available here

    • Tickets

  • How much does it cost?

    There are three types of tickets to choose from, which are avaliable on the ticketing page.

    Note that General Admission tickets do not include tastings unless otherwise listed.

  • How do I purchase tickets?

    Tickets can be purchased through Oztix, and at select cellar doors in Tasmania.

  • Can I purchase tickets on the door?

    If there is still tickets available (expect these sessions to sell out), these will be available for purchase. Tickets on the door are sold at a premium.

  • Do I need to print my tickets?

    No, tickets purchased from Oztix can be scanned from your phone.

  • I brought the wrong ticket!

    Don’t worry, we can help!
    Please go to the ticketing page and purchase the right tickets. After you have done so you can contact Oztix to refund the wrongly purchased ticket. Please get in touch with the Oztix Customer Care team via info@oztix.com.au

    • The Event

  • What do I get?

    • Access to Tasmania's best vineyards in one location
    • Heaps of local food options
    • Live music from great local bands & artists
    • Premium reuseable wine glass

    Purchasing a Tasting Ticket brings the benefits of a General Admission ticket, along with 4x Tasting Tokens.

  • What does a VIP ticket include

    VIP tickets to the Tasmanian Wine Festival - Winter Edition includes:
    • Access to Tasmania's best vineyards in one location
    • Heaps of local food options
    • Live music from great local bands & artists
    • Premium reuseable wine glass
    • 4x Tasting Tokens
    • Glass of bubbles on arrival
    • Access to the Frogmore Creek Luxury Lounge


    VIP Tickets are 18+

  • Is this an all ages event?

    The Winter Edition of the Tasmanian Wine Festival has two All Ages Sessions: Saturday Afternoon & Sunday.

    As this is a licensed event, patrons under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian above the age of 25.

    Ticket holders under the age of 18 cannot purchase a Tasting or VIP ticket.

  • What can't I bring?

    • Glassware
    • Alcohol
    • Animals
    • Dangerous goods or illegal substances
    • Food - We have a wide range of food options available at the event
    • We ask that you are patient upon entry while bag checks are undertaken.

  • Are white wines and bubbles available at the Winter Edition?

    Yes!

    Besides a great selection of reds, there will be plenty of beautiful whites and bubbles to choose from.

  • Is there food avalaible?

    Yes. We have a beautiful range of food available, also including vegan, vegetarian, gluten free and dairy free options.

  • Are there beverages avalaible other than wine?

    We have a wide variety of beverages available from craft beers, to ciders and cocktails. This is in addition to a wide range of non alcoholic drinks.

  • What COVID-19 Safety Measures are in place?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival meets all current regulatory requirements to ensure that this event is COVIDSafe. A COVID-19 Safety Plan has been implemented to ensure that the festival can proceed safely.

    • Tastings

  • Are there tastings?

    Most vineyards at the Tasmanian Wine Festival offer tastings to patrons. Tastings can be redeemed by purchasing Tasting Tokens that are avalaible on the day, or included with a Tasting Ticket or VIP Ticket.

    There are no free tastings at the Tasmanian Wine Festival.

  • Are there free tastings?

    No. You can buy tasting tokens on the day.

  • How much do tastings cost?

    Tasting tokens are avalaible in the Tasting & VIP tickets, and are also avalaible for purchase at the event. Tastings are $3 each or $10 for 4.
