Frequently Asked Questions

    When & Where

  • Where's the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held at the Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens in Hobart, Tasmania.

    Access to the event is via the main gates on Lower Domain Road.

  • When is the event?

    The Tasmanian Wine Festival is held over three sessions during Mar 18 & 19, 2023.

    Saturday Afternoon: 12.00pm - 4.00pm
    Saturday Evening: 5.30pm - 10.00pm
    Sunday Afternoon 12.00pm - 5.00pm

    Tickets are available here

    • Tickets

  • How much does it cost?

    Check out the ticketing page for pricing.

  • How do I purchase tickets?

    Tickets can be purchased through Oztix, and at select cellar doors in Tasmania.

    Tickets should only purchased through the authorised ticket provider. Tickets brought from any other platform cannot be guaranteed as legitimate.

    Patrons with fraudulent tickets will not be admitted.

  • Can I purchase tickets on the door?

    If there are still tickets available, these will be available for purchase.

    At previous editions, multiple sessions sold out prior to the event, so pre-purchasing tickets is highly recommended.

    Tickets on the door are sold at a premium.

  • Do I need to print my tickets?

    Nope, to help save paper, we prefer digital tickets! Have your tickets ready on your phone to scan.

  • Do you accept companion cards?

    Yep! The Tasmanian Wine Festival accepts valid Companion Cards. We encourage Companion Card holders to advise the festival before arrival.info@taswinefest.com.au

    • The Event

  • What do I get?

    • Access to Tasmania's best vineyards in one location
    • Heaps of local food options
    • Live music from great bands & artists
    • Premium reuseable wine glass

    Purchasing a Tasting Ticket brings the benefits of a General Admission ticket, along with 3x Tasting Tokens.

  • What does a Tasting ticket include

    Tasting Tickets to the Tasmanian Wine Festival include general admission to the festival and 3x tasting tokens upon arrival.

    Tasting Tickets are 18+

  • What does a VIP ticket include

    Check out the ticketing page for VIP ticket inclusions

    VIP Tickets are 18+

  • Is this an all ages event?

    The Saturday Afternoon and Sunday sessions are Licensed All Ages, all other sessions are 18+.

    Patrons under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian above the age of 25.

    Children aged between 5-17 years must hold a ticket to gain entry. Under 5s are free and do not require a ticket.

    Ticket holders under the age of 18 cannot purchase a Tasting or VIP ticket.

  • Is there food available?

    Yes. We have a beautiful range of food available, also including vegan, vegetarian, gluten free and dairy free options.

  • What can't I bring?

    A list of items that can't enter the festival will be published closer to the event.

  • Are there beverages available other than wine?

    We have a wide variety of beverages available from craft beers, to ciders and cocktails. This is in addition to a wide range of non alcoholic drinks.

    • Masterclasses

  • What are Masterclasses?

    Masterclasses are intimate wine focused classes bring together the best experts in Tasmania. Previous hosts have included Joseph Burton (Tasmania’s first certified Sommelier), Carl Winsdor (Willing Bros.), and representivies from Clover Hill Wines and Frogmore Creek. Each Masterclass session will get tastings of a handpicked selection of Tasmania’s wines, paired with a gourmet selection of Bruny Island Oysters, cheeses and other delights. Learn from the best in the field and savour your senses.

    Get your Masterclass tickets

  • Are Masterclasses 18+

    Yes, entry into the Masterclass area is only for guests aged over 18.

  • How long is each Masterclass

    Each Masterclass goes for approximately 25 minutes

  • Where are the Masterclasses held?

    The Masterclass sessions are held within the Tasmanian Wine Festival. This hidden location will be published closer to the event.

  • Do I need a ticket to the Tasmanian Wine Festival

    Yes, the Masterclasses are an add-on to your Tasmanian Wine Festival experience. Ticket purchasers must hold a ticket to the same Tasmanian Wine Festival session as their chosen Masterclass.

    • Tastings

  • Are there tastings?

    Most vineyards at the Tasmanian Wine Festival offer tastings to patrons. Tastings can be redeemed by purchasing Tasting Tokens that are available on the day, or included with a Tasting Ticket or VIP Ticket.

    Like what you tried? Make sure to purchase a glass or bottle to support Tasmanian wine producers & vineyards!

    There are no free tastings at the Tasmanian Wine Festival.

  • Are there free tastings?

    No. Vendors at the Tasmanian Wine Festival does not offer free tastings.

    Tasting Tokens are avalaible with either a Tasting or VIP Ticket, or can be purchased at the event.

    Like what you tried? Make sure to purchase a glass or bottle to support Tasmanian wine producers & vineyards!
